Known Facts, Signs and Symptoms

Hover over the cards below to find out more about the four types of EDs





Anorexia (an-o-REK-see-uh) Nervosa



Engages in purging activities such as self induced vomiting and using laxatives

Signs: extreme restrcted eating, extreme thiness (emaciation)

Binge Eating Disorder



Includes periods of uncontrolled, impulsive and continous eating till being uncomfortably full

The aftermath involves guilt and self loathing

Bulimia (boo-LEE-me-uh) Nervosa



Is normally a repeated cycle of binging and purging

Signs: Consuming a large amount of food before engaging in self induced vomiting and extreme excercising

Orthorexia Nervosa



Obsession with eating what is perceived to be healthy

Victims of Otheroxia mainly seeking to have "clean bodies" by consuming and intaking only healthy foods
